LokInLab Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

LokInLab ("we", "our", or "us") provides a focus timer and habit tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed when you use LokInLab.

Summary

  • LokInLab works without account sign-in.
  • Core progress data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not collect precise location data.

Data We Process

Data stored locally on your device

LokInLab stores the following app progress values locally using on-device storage:

  • Habit days and session count
  • Daily streak value
  • Weekly streak value
  • Last completion day and week markers
  • Tier progression derived from the values above

This local storage is used only to provide app features.

Data not collected by default

In the current app version, LokInLab does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect:

  • Name or email
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Photos or files
  • Precise location
  • Payment card information

Notifications

LokInLab can send local device notifications for session completion.

  • Notification content is generated on-device.
  • No notification content is sent to our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share your local progress data with third parties in the current app version.

If third-party services are added in future releases, including analytics, crash reporting, cloud sync, or ads, this policy will be updated before those features are enabled.

Data Retention

Local app data remains on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect app integrity. No method of storage is 100% secure, but we aim to minimize data exposure by keeping progress data on-device.

Children's Privacy

LokInLab is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your Choices

  • Disable notifications in device settings
  • Reset progress through app behavior and features
  • Remove app data by uninstalling or clearing app storage

International Users

If LokInLab is offered globally, local laws may provide additional rights. Contact us using the details below for requests.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when features or legal requirements change. Updated versions will include a revised effective date.
